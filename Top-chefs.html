Appetizing Pizza

Top Chefs

Franco Pepe

Italy

July 18, 1963

Known for his pizzeria "Pepe in Grani" in Caiazzo, Italy, Franco Pepe is celebrated for his traditional Neapolitan pizzas made with meticulous attention to ingredients and technique.

Not all pizzas are made equal, but those that grace Franco Pepe's pizza oven are the best in the world. His family have been baking in Campania for three generations, and his pizza restaurant is now internationally recognised.

Nancy Silverton

Los Angeles, California, United States

June 20, 1954

Nancy Silverton, a James Beard Award-winning chef, co-founded "Pizzeria Mozza" in Los Angeles and Newport Beach as well as Osteria Mozza, Mozza2Go and chi SPACCA in Los Angeles.. She is renowned for her artisanal approach to pizza dough and inventive topping combinations.

Silverton also founded the world-renowned La Brea Bakery as well as Campanile Restaurant, an institution that Angelenos cherished for decades.

Enzo Coccia

Italy

1962

Born in 1962 and a contagious passion that began when, still a child, he used to help his father, Antonio, in the family pizzeria. Grown up in the working-class district of Duchesca, behind Piazza Garibaldi and the Central Station, he soon realized that the profession of pizzaiolo was what he wanted to do in his life. After a few years, he decided to take on a first challenge opening the doors of his pizzeria, or better pizzAria, as in the old way, La Notizia.

Enzo Coccia is a master of Neapolitan pizza, operating his renowned pizzeria "La Notizia" in Naples. He emphasizes using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients and has helped popularize traditional Neapolitan pizza worldwide.
